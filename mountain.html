Mountain Oracle bone script was an ancestor of modern Chinese characters engraved on oracle bones, and is the earliest known form of Chinese writing. The oracle bone appears pictographic, its simplified, and rounded forms are often converted to rectilinear ones. The Oracle bone script are often arranged so that the columns begin near the centerline of the shell or bone, and move toward the edge, such that the two sides are ordered in mirror-image fashion.
